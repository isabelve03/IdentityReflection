About Me

My name is Isabel Veliz, a game developer with a passion for creativity and storytelling.

From coding projects to soundtracks, from art to engaging narratives, I find that all these elements create meaningful experiences that truly speak to me.

The reason why I fell in love with video games from a young age because it contains all aspects of art which forms emotional connections and conveys a story.

My Story

I was always a creative child at heart. I constantly spent hours drawing and sketching the environment around me.

I played a lot of instruments since I was young: Violin, piano, ukelele, and both acoustic and electric guitar.

Writing also allowed me to build the imaginative worlds to word and further express myself.


Starting young, I have always loved video games.

I found that games combined all of my hobbies into one and wanted to create experiences for others.

Currently, I am pursuing a bachelor's in Computer Science with an emphasis in game development.

Eevery academic projeect is an opportunity to mergee coding with my creativity.


Games are an art in themselves, similar to the expression of film, video games gives a deeper personal connection to the character one plays as.

In my career, I plan to create experiences and connect others to as a community similar to what I had growing up.

Faith Integration

My faith is shown through my empathy and drive for connection.

I have always sought to bring comfort to those around me. As a creation of God, I am the extension of love and compassion through storytelling.

Video games has always been a source of both community and solace by immersing myself into these different characters, worlds, and storylines.

I aim to create worlds that inspire comfort, lessons, and a change of exploration for self-discoveery in imaginative ways through games.
